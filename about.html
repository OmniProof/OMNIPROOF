“Omni” means universal — we go cross-chain, cross-culture, cross-meta.
“Proof” means we don’t talk. We show.

This project was inspired by @RProof_tech and their mission to bring credibility back to degen culture. But we’re here to take that even further — with style, grit, and community-first fire.
